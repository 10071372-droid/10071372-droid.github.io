ABOUT About Yiwen

I am Yiwen (Ollie) Zhao, an interdisciplinary designer with a background in architecture and design engineering. My practice moves between spatial research, healthcare environments, disability & accessibility, and interactive storytelling. I am interested in how design can reframe everyday systems — from hospital rehabilitation spaces to digital interfaces — to better support vulnerable bodies and diverse ways of experiencing the world.
